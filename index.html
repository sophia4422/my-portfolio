My Interests

My other creative projects

Oil Painting

I have always enjoyed drawing but during the lockdown in 2020 I tried portrait painting using oil paint. This painting (unfinished) is of Rihanna; it is currently on display at the Bertie Crewe Gallery in the Palace Theatre, Redditch.

Animation

During my A-levels, I designed and animated some revision videos using Adobe Flash, Photoshop and Illustrator. I started making them to help with my own learning and I asked my friends to narrate them; they ended up helping people from all over the world and my most viewed video has 97k views!

Design
